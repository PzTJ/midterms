Frequently Asked Questions

Ordering & Cart

How do I add items to my cart?

To add items, please visit our Products page. Click on any product you're interested in to see more details or options. Once you've selected the size (if applicable) and quantity in the pop-up window or product page, click the "Add to Cart" button. Your item will then appear in the cart panel!

How can I change item quantities in my cart?

Quantity adjustments can be made on the main Cart page (cart.html). Each item listed in your cart will have '+' and '-' buttons next to its quantity, allowing you to increase or decrease the amount easily. You can also type a number directly into the quantity box.

How do I remove an item from my cart?

On both the Products page side-cart and the main Cart page, each item listed has a small 'Ã—' button. Clicking this button will remove the item directly from your cart.

Can I order deals like "Night For 2" more than once?

Currently, our system limits deals like "Night For 2" and "Solo Watcher" to one per order to ensure availability. However, you can adjust the quantity for the "Seasoned Popcorn Maker" deal directly in your cart!

I added an item but don't see it in the cart panel.

Please ensure you clicked the "Add to Cart" button after selecting your options in the pop-up window or product page. If the issue persists, try refreshing the page. The cart uses your browser's localStorage, which should update instantly.

Does the cart save if I close the browser?

Yes, items added to your cart are saved in your browser's localStorage, so they should still be there if you close and reopen the browser. However, this is specific to the browser and computer you used.

Products & Options

What sizes are available for popcorn?

Our standard popcorn flavors (Buttery Classic, Caramel Bliss, Spicy Chili, Cheesy Cheddar) are available in 64oz, 105oz, and 170oz.

What sizes do the drinks come in?

All our soft drinks (Cola, Iced Tea, Orange Juice, Water) come in 21oz, 30oz, and 42oz sizes.

Do the DIY Kits have different sizes?

No, our DIY Kits come in one standard size and do not have size options.

Are the DIY Kits suitable for beginners?

Yes! The Popcorn Kernel Kit simply provides quality kernels for popping using your preferred method (stovetop, air popper, etc.). The Seasoning Sampler lets you easily experiment with different flavors on your finished popcorn.

Where can I find more detailed information about specific products?

For our Popcorn, Kernel Kit, and Seasoning Sampler, you can click the "View Full Details" link found on their respective pop-up details window (accessed from the Products page) to visit their dedicated pages.

Account (Simulated)

How is my account data stored?

For this website project, user account details (username, password, phone number) are stored directly in your browser's `localStorage`. This is a simulation technique and means the data is only stored on your current computer and browser; it is not sent to or stored on a central server.

What happens if I "Forget Password"?

Since this is a simulation, the "Forgot Password" link provides an option to clear all simulated account data currently stored in your browser's `localStorage`. This allows you to 'reset' the accounts for testing purposes.

What happens when I log out?

Logging out clears your logged-in status for the current browser session (sessionStorage). Your account information remains saved in localStorage so you can log back in later on the same browser.
