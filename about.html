The Story

It all started with a simple love for that magical movie snack: popcorn! From experimenting with family recipes in our home kitchen in Hanoi to sharing our creations at local markets, our passion grew. We dreamed of creating a place dedicated to perfecting popcorn, offering classic flavors alongside exciting new twists.

In 2023, that dream became a reality when we opened our first small shop. Our mission is simple: to pop fresh, delicious, high-quality popcorn that brings a little bit of joy to your day. We believe in great ingredients, friendly service, and the simple happiness a good bowl of popcorn can bring.
