Join Our Poppin' Team!

We're passionate about popcorn and creating happy customer experiences. If you're enthusiastic, reliable, and love a fun atmosphere, check out our current openings below!

Sales Clerk

Location: Hanoi Store | Type: Full-time/Part-time

Responsibilities: Greeting customers, taking orders accurately, handling cash and card transactions, keeping the counter area clean and stocked, answering questions about flavors and products, and ensuring a positive customer experience.

Requirements: Friendly and outgoing personality, good communication skills, basic math skills, ability to work in a fast-paced environment, previous retail or customer service experience preferred but not required.

Social Media Manager

Location: Remote/Hanoi | Type: Part-time Contract

Responsibilities: Managing our brand presence across platforms like Facebook, Instagram, TikTok. Creating engaging content (photos, videos, posts) showcasing our products and promotions. Responding to customer comments and messages online. Tracking analytics and reporting on performance.

Requirements: Proven experience managing social media accounts for a brand, strong understanding of current social media trends, excellent written and visual communication skills, creative mindset, basic photo/video editing skills.

Popcorn Mascot Performer

Location: Hanoi Events/Store | Type: Part-time/Weekend

Responsibilities: Bringing our brand mascot ("Kernel Cobb"?) to life! Performing in costume at the store and local events. Engaging with customers, posing for photos, creating a fun and energetic atmosphere. Must be comfortable in costume for extended periods.

Requirements: High energy and enthusiasm, enjoys interacting with people of all ages, physically able to perform in costume, reliable transportation, previous performance or mascot experience is a plus.

Popcorn Maker / Kitchen Staff

Location: Hanoi Store | Type: Full-time/Part-time

Responsibilities: Operating popcorn machines safely and efficiently, preparing different popcorn flavors according to recipes, ensuring consistent quality and freshness, managing ingredient inventory (kernels, oil, seasonings), maintaining kitchen cleanliness and hygiene standards.

Requirements: Ability to follow recipes accurately, attention to detail, ability to stand for extended periods, understanding of food safety practices, previous kitchen or food prep experience beneficial.
